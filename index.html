Why We Built Pontis

During my time as a Technical Lead at a major tech company managing CRM & Compliance architecture, I saw a recurring nightmare. We had brilliant teams spending thousands of hours trying to automate incredibly rigid, complex web workflows.

The standard answer was always "use an API" or "buy an RPA tool." But the reality of enterprise software is that APIs are often missing, broken, or locked down. Legacy RPA tools (like the screen-scrapers of the past) were so fragile that a single button color change could bring a quarterly compliance sync to a grinding halt. We spent more time fixing bots than writing them.

Even worse, the new wave of "Cloud AI Agents" fundamentally misunderstood enterprise security. You cannot send session tokens, MFA cookies, and PII to an external cloud provider's API. It's a non-starter for SOC 2, HIPAA, or strict GDPR compliance.

Pontis is the platform I wished I had.

We built it to be a true "local loop." It runs on your hardware, within your authenticated sessions. It doesn't break when a selector changes; it pauses, asks you for help, and instantly rewrites its own Markdown skill code to heal the entire fleet in seconds. It's not just an automation tool; it's a resilient, secure AI colleague.
